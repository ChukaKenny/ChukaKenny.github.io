Slack: Silent Notification Drop on Live Mobile App
Identified a critical production defect in Slack's live mobile app where notifications selectively fail to deliver, even with all alert settings configured to show at all times. Teams miss channel updates and direct messages with no indication anything was dropped. Found through deliberate exploratory testing on the production app, not a controlled environment. At scale, this quietly erodes the core value proposition Slack is built on: reliable communication.
Silent failure · All alert settings on · User trust impact · Core product risk

🟠 Severity: High🎬
Loom: Video Limit Bypass via Temp Mail + VPN Chain
Found a production exploit allowing unlimited free videos on a free-tier account (25-video limit) by chaining private browsing, VPN rotation, and temp mail services. Exploited to 125 videos voluntarily; the ceiling was open-ended. On a subscription model, this is a direct revenue bypass at scale. The kind of real-world abuse path that only surfaces through adversarial exploratory thinking, not scripted test cases. Voluntarily disclosed.
125 free videos obtained · Subscription bypass · Revenue at risk · Abuse path open
